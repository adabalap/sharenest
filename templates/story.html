For Architects: A Scalable & Secure Blueprint

ShareNest's architecture is meticulously designed for high performance, resilience, and cost-efficiency on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure.

Simplified diagram of ShareNest's OCI-centric architecture.

Key Architectural Principles:

  • **Direct-to-Cloud Uploads:** Leveraging OCI Pre-Authenticated Requests (PARs) for direct client-to-storage uploads, minimizing server load.
  • **Modular & Microservice-Oriented:** Clear separation of concerns for easier development, deployment, and scaling.
  • **Performance & Scalability:** Designed for high throughput and elasticity, handling varying loads with grace.
  • **Efficient Data Management:** Automated cleanup and OCI lifecycle policies for optimal storage utilization.
