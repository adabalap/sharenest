ShareNest: Securely Share Your World.

Effortless, Private, and Powered by the Cloud.

The Challenge of Digital Sharing.

In an age of constant connectivity, ensuring the privacy and security of your shared data is paramount. Traditional methods often compromise control, leaving your files vulnerable to unauthorized access or indefinite retention.

ShareNest rises to this challenge by providing a platform where **you** dictate who, when, and how your files are accessed. We offer a simple, secure, and controlled alternative to reclaim your digital autonomy.

Built on Trust, Fueled by Community.

ShareNest embraces the open-source philosophy, promoting transparency and collaborative innovation. Our codebase is openly accessible, allowing developers and security experts worldwide to scrutinize, contribute, and enhance the platform.

This commitment to open source ensures a trustworthy, secure, and continuously evolving application, free from hidden agendas and proprietary lock-ins. Join our community and help shape the future of secure sharing.

Engineered for Performance & Security.

ShareNest's robust architecture is designed for scalability, speed, and uncompromised security. At its core, we leverage direct-to-cloud file operations, significantly offloading our application server and boosting efficiency.

Our innovative approach includes Pre-Authenticated Requests (PARs) for direct client-to-cloud uploads and downloads, eliminating bottlenecks and ensuring blazing-fast performance, even for large files.
